\# Component Specifications



\## Arduino UNO

\- MCU: ATmega328P, 16 MHz, 5V, 14 digital pins, 6 analog pins



\## HC-SR04 — Ultrasonic Sensor

\- Range: 2–400 cm | Accuracy: ±3 mm | Trigger: 10µs pulse



\## UV Sensor Module

\- Output: Digital HIGH (bright) / LOW (dark)

\- Voltage: 3.3V–5V



\## LM35 — Temperature Sensor

\- Output: 10 mV/°C analog | Range: −55°C to +150°C



\## MQ-2 — Smoke/Gas Sensor

\- Detects: LPG, smoke, alcohol, methane

\- Output: Analog 0–1023 | Warm-up: \~60 seconds



\## Active Buzzer

\- Voltage: 3.5V–5V | Generates sound internally when powered



\## LEDs

| Color | Meaning | Resistor |

|---|---|---|

| Red | Smoke/Fire alert | 220Ω |

| Yellow | High temperature | 220Ω |

| Green | All clear / safe | 220Ω |
